Which transport to choose?

Giving meaning to your trip
No need to take a plane to change universe. Thanks to its astonishing diversity, Europe offers a change of scenery within easy reach by train. Another big advantage: stations are generally located in the heart of cities, making travel easier. In addition, many high-speed lines criss-cross the continent. Finally, trains are often more convenient, faster, cheaper and obviously less polluting than cars. The ideal way to travel in complete freedom!
